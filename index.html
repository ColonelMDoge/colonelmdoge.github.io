MY "EXPERIMENTS"

Click Till 10!

This was a small game from 2022 when I was into the Stanley Parable. I actually had motivation at the time. My biggest challenge was creating the text engine (which sucks) and a story to justify clicking ten times. I was also into the Serializable interface, so there's that I guess. Design wise, it could be way better, but the story is peak.

RPG Dialogue Handler

Back when I had hopes and dreams, I wanted to make a story about a hero going across multiple dimensions to retrieve fragments of stolen shards that powered a barrier to stop an oncoming asteroid. This is one example of dialogue that could have happened between the protagonist and one of the guardians that protected the shard for altruistic and monetary gain.

PNC Tilesheet Editor

When I was into tile based games, I wanted to create an application to help me use tilesheets from the internet and convert them into tilesets for me to build my own maps. It also can join tiles into a singular tilesheet, and can split an image into pieces to stuff on Mindustry screens hahaha. Major inspiration from a Reddit post online because I cannot design anything. Making this gave me the hard reality of dealing with LayoutManagers.

PNCBot

So what does PNC even mean? It stands for the three Minecraft usernames of my bros and I when we grind CurseForge modpacks together. The bot is capable of playing music online to solve the problem of unsupported music bots. It can also scrape citations and I added a Groq AI thing to pretend that the bot has intelligence to make it cooler. Still thinking how to allow it to handle TTS and STT though; that would be peak.

Java SavedModel Tester

I was having so much fun with Teachable Machine when I realized that Java support was limited. The internet barely had any information, so I had to scrape what I could find to create a GUI that can test its models. Unfortunately, only the image recognition is compatible with Java. The fun thing about this is that I can link the model outputs to keybinds, so I can basically control Snake or Pac-Man with my hands. Pretty cool, huh?
